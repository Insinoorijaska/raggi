Kyriakos Grizzly (Kyriakos Kapakoulak) is an exceptionally strong, viral internet strength athlete known for unique, high-volume, and heavy eccentric training rather than standard powerlifting. Weighing over 200 kg (440+ lbs), he is famous for performing 14 reps of 200 kg on pad benches and 440 kg Zercher holds. Key strength metrics and training habits include:
Unique Lifting Style: Grizzly is known for high-intensity, unconventional movements, such as heavy partial reps, high-pulls, and intense, short-range movements.
Pad Benches: 14 reps with 200 kg (440 lbs).
Zercher Holds: Holds up to 440 kg (970 lbs).
Bodyweight & Build: Stands around 6'1" and weighs roughly 212 kg (467 lbs), often maxing out standard scales.
Experience: He has a background as an Olympic weightlifter and powerlifter, boasting significant strength despite training around injuries. His training often focuses on isometric holds and extreme weight, which has made him a notable figure in the "strongest man on the internet" conversation.
