Everyone should focus on compound, functional movements that build strength, mobility, and cardiovascular health. Key exercises include squats, deadlifts, lunges, push-ups, pulling exercises (rows/pull-ups), overhead presses, and loaded carries. These movements maximize efficiency by working multiple muscle groups simultaneously. 

Essential Gym Exercises for Everyone
Squats: Targets legs, glutes, and core.
Deadlifts: Builds posterior chain strength.
Lunges: Improves leg strength, balance, and stability.
Push-ups/Bench Press: Targets chest, shoulders, and triceps.
Pulling Exercises (Rows/Pull-ups): Strengthens back and biceps.
Overhead Press: Builds shoulder strength.
Loaded Carries (Farmer's Walk): Improves grip strength and core stability.
Planks/Core Work: Enhances core stability. 

Key Considerations
Compound Movement Focus: Prioritize exercises that work multiple muscle groups.
Balance: Include both pushing and pulling movements to maintain joint health and symmetry.
Consistency & Form: Focus on proper form to prevent injury and ensure effectiveness.
Mobility & Cardio: Incorporate stretching and cardiovascular training to support overall health. 

For beginners, starting with bodyweight versions of these exercises is recommended, gradually increasing weight and intensity.
